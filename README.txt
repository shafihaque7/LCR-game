This is a lrc or lcr game. Each players are given 3 dollars. Rolling a L means the player gives 1 dollar to the left. Rolling a R means the player gives 1 dollar to the right. Rolling a C means the player puts 1 dollar in the pot. Rolling a dot means the player gets a pass. The player gets to roll the die as long as he or she has money. 

To compile simply type "make" then "./lrc". It will ask for the seed. Simply type a random
number. Then it will ask for the number of players. Type the number of players ranging from 2 to 10. It will announce the name of the winner with the amount left in the bank and the amount in the pot which the winner also takes.
